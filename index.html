How the Idea Was Born

It all started with a random Reddit post I stumbled upon months ago. Someone suggested using gaming failures as workout motivation - every death equals an exercise. The idea stuck with me.

Soon after, I found myself keeping a notepad next to my PC while playing War Thunder. Every time I died in air combat without scoring points, I'd jot it down. Over time, I developed a rotation system to ensure balanced workouts.

How the Website Works

This automates my manual notepad system. Each death adds one exercise following a strict rotation: Arms → Chest → Back → Abs → Legs → Repeat. The system remembers your position between sessions, ensuring you can't skip muscle groups.
